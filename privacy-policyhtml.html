Privacy Policy

FICO Academy Gulf | Last updated: June 2024

FICO Academy Gulf ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our SAP FICO training application.

1. Information We Collect

When you register and use FICO Academy Gulf, we collect:

  • Full name and email address (for account creation)
  • Password (stored locally on your device, never on our servers)
  • Learning progress (lessons completed, quiz scores)
  • Account type (free or premium)

2. How We Store Your Data

All user data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage on your own device. We do not operate external servers or databases. Your personal information never leaves your device unless you choose to contact us directly.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your learning account
  • Track your course progress and quiz scores
  • Issue your Certificate of Completion
  • Provide premium content access after payment verification

4. Payment Information

We do not collect or store any payment card details. All payments are handled directly between the student and the instructor via WhatsApp, bank transfer, or third-party payment platforms (Wise, PayPal). We have no access to your financial information.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties. Your data remains private and is only used for the purposes described in this policy.

6. Children's Privacy

FICO Academy Gulf is designed for adult learners (18+) in professional finance and accounting fields. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies. We only use browser localStorage to save your login session and learning progress on your own device.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data at any time
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Request information about how your data is used
  • Opt out of any communications

To delete your data, simply clear your browser's localStorage or uninstall the app.

9. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Since all data is stored locally on your device, security depends on your device's own security measures. We recommend using a strong password for your account.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with a new "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of the United Arab Emirates. Any disputes shall be subject to the jurisdiction of UAE courts.
